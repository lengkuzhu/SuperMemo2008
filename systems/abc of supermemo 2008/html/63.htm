Subject: Topic #63: Converting extracts to questions and answers
You can convert fragments of articles to questions and answers. You will do it using the tool called Cloze. A cloze is a sentence with a word missing and replaced with three dots. Such a sentence can be understood as a question. Your job is to recall the missing part of the sentence. For example:

Sentence: The theory of relativity was proposed by Einstein


Cloze:
     Question: The theory of relativity was proposed by [...]
     Answer: Einstein

Topic #65: Cloze tools (8 elements)

To convert a sentence into a cloze do the following:

  1. Select a meaningful keyword in the sentence (e.g. by double-clicking it with the mouse)
  2. Press Alt+Z, or
    choose Reading : Remember cloze on the context menu (right-click), or
    click any of Z-icons on the read toolbar (see the picture on the right)

To see the newly created cloze question, press Alt+Left or choose the back button ()
