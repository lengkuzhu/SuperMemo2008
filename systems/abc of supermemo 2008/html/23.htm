Subject: Topic #23: Repetition cycle

To make sure you remember well, SuperMemo will test your memory. Each little test will include these steps:

  1. Answering a question (mentally or verbally)
  2. Clicking Show answer (or Enter) to see the correct answer
  3. Comparing your response (from Step 1) with the answer displayed on the screen (in Step 2)
  4. Choosing a grade reflecting how well you remember (e.g. Good if you remember, Fail if you do not remember, etc.)
  5. Clicking Next repetition (or Enter)

Let's test it on an example (press Enter)

Item #24: Exemplary repetition

Which year was the United Nations established?

Hint: Look at the question above and try to answer it (mentally or verbally). Choose Show answer (or press Enter) to see the correct answer

1945

Hint: Compare your response with the correct answer: 1945. Choose a grade: e.g. Bright if you answered 1945, or Fail if you answered 1935. Press Enter to continue (optional reading explains grades: Learning with SuperMemo)
