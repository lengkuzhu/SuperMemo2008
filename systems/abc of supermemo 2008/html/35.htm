Subject: Topic #35: 95% retention

If you remember to make all your repetitions every day, SuperMemo will make sure you remember 95% of the learned material.

You can increase this number at the cost of the speed of learning. You can also learn faster but remember less.
For more details see: Forgetting index at supermemo.com

Rarely, your material may be so difficult that SuperMemo will not be able to guarantee 95% recall. To prevent this from happening read: 20 rules of formulating knowledge at supermemo.com

Topic #36: Remember! If you complete your repetitions every day (button Learn), SuperMemo will ... (2 elements)

Remember! If you complete your repetitions every day (button Learn), SuperMemo will make sure you remember 95% of the learned material. Only a tiny fraction of your learning material will be presented for review daily. This fraction is compulsory if you want to ensure 95% retention.

Item #39: Repetitio mater studiorum est (top)

How many times does SuperMemo ask you a given question (like this one)? (click the correct answer)
False: only once (you never see the question again)
False: several times on a single day (and never again in the future)
False: as often as possible (if you only have patience to answer)
True: several times in the future (e.g. 10 times in a lifetime)
False: as many times as you want to be asked
